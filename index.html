The Workshop

Where Every Cut Is Intentional

⸻

Edge & Awl was born from a simple belief: that the objects we carry every day should be made to last a lifetime. Each piece is cut, stitched, and finished by hand in our small workshop — no shortcuts, no machines doing the work that hands should do.

We source full-grain vegetable-tanned leather from traditional tanneries, use waxed linen thread for every stitch, and finish every edge by hand. The result is leather that only gets better with age.

01

Full-Grain Leather

Only the top layer of the hide — the strongest, most durable surface.

02

Saddle Stitching

Two-needle hand stitching that won't unravel if a single thread breaks.

03

Burnished Edges

Every edge slicked, beveled, and finished — the mark of quality work.
